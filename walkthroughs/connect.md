## Connect Your Anypoint Account

Anypoint Monitor uses **OAuth 2.0** to securely connect to your Anypoint Platform.

### What happens when you connect:

1. A browser window opens for Anypoint Platform authentication
2. After login, the extension securely stores your tokens
3. Your environments and permissions are fetched automatically
4. The sidebar updates with your account information

### Multi-Account Support

You can connect **multiple** Anypoint Platform organizations and switch between them seamlessly using the Account Manager.

> **Tip:** Your credentials are stored securely using VS Code's built-in SecretStorage API — never in plain text.
